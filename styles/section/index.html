Section

Flexible layout component that groups content and components together providing consistent vertical spacing.

Usage

A section provides a selection of background colour and style options to help you build and design.

Do's:

  • Use it with or without a container.
  • Use it inside the grid.
  • Organise a group of components together that provide functionality for the user.
